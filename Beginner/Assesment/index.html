About us

This the official web site of alumni association of vavuniya pavatkulam ganesuwara maha viththiyalayam. The association has been founded on 2020 by the group of old students at the school main hall with the participation principal. According the to constitution the principal would be the chairman of the association and all other members would be selected from the old students. you can find the current secretary of the association from the contact as page.
